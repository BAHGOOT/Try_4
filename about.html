The Faculty of Engineering at Shoubra, part of Benha University in Egypt, was established in 1976 through a presidential decree that merged the Higher Institute of Art in Shubra with Ain Shams University. It became an independent faculty under Benha University in 2005. The faculty is located in West Shubra Al Khaimah, Qalyubia Governorate.
